The Buying Process

From first contact to your machine live in its location — here's what to expect.

1

Inquiry

Contact us via the website, WhatsApp, or phone. Tell us your location, what products you plan to sell, and how many machines you need.

2

Advance Payment (50%)

Pay half the machine price to confirm your order. This is when the clock starts — your machine will be ready in 3–4 weeks.

3

Initial Demo

We show you how the machine works. You see the spring layout, coil sizes, and how products dispense before customization begins.

4

Customization

This is your chance to configure exactly what your machine holds:

  • • Wide products (chips, Kurkure) → double spring
  • • Thin products → narrow, more slots per row
  • • Square base products → wide, fewer slots per row
  • • You choose the product mix — we configure the springs to match

Changing springs after purchase incurs an additional fee. Get it right here.

5

Full Demo

Your customized machine runs a complete demo with your actual products. You confirm everything works as expected.

6

Final Payment

Pay the remaining 50%. We accept Fonepay, NepalpPay, and bank transfer. EMI through our banking partners can be arranged.

7

Delivery

We deliver anywhere in Nepal — as far as a bus goes. You can also arrange your own vehicle (and skip the transport fee). Optional stickering and packaging are done before dispatch.

8

Installation & You're Live

Our team installs the machine at your site. We guide you through operation via YouTube tutorials, WhatsApp, and video calls. Your machine is ready to earn.
