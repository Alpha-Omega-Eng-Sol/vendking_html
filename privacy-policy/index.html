Privacy Policy

Vending King App — by Vending King Pvt. Ltd.

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Vending King Pvt. Ltd. ("Vending King", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Vending King mobile application (the "App"), which allows operators to remotely monitor and manage IphaVend smart vending machines. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have in relation to your data.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the App:

Account Information

Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register for an operator account.

Machine & Business Data

Vending machine identifiers, placement locations, product listings, inventory levels, pricing configurations, and transaction records generated by your machines.

Location Data

Approximate or precise GPS coordinates of vending machines you register, and optionally your device location if you enable location-based machine tracking.

Device & Usage Information

Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, IP address, app version, crash logs, and feature-usage patterns collected automatically to improve app stability and performance.

Communications

Messages or feedback you send to our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its IoT features.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Deliver real-time sales notifications, alerts, and inventory updates to your devices.
  • Generate profit analytics, purchase logs, and performance summaries for your vending portfolio.
  • Diagnose technical issues, crashes, and machine connectivity problems.
  • Send operational communications such as account confirmations, password resets, and service announcements.
  • Improve and develop new features based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.
  • Comply with legal obligations applicable in Nepal and relevant jurisdictions.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

Trusted third-party vendors (cloud hosting, analytics, push notifications) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations.

Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Vending King, our users, or the public.

Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

With Your Consent

For any other purpose with your explicit prior consent.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational safeguards — including encrypted data transmission (TLS), secure cloud storage, and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and machine data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. Transaction logs and analytics data are retained for up to 3 years to support business reporting and legal compliance. You may request deletion of your account at any time (see Section 6). After deletion, residual data may be kept in anonymised, aggregated form.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

👁️

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

✏️

Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.

🗑️

Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated personal data.

📤

Portability

Request an export of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

🚫

Opt-out of Marketing

Unsubscribe from non-operational communications at any time.

📍

Location Permissions

Revoke device location access at any time via your phone's settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@vendking.org. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Push Notifications

The App sends push notifications for sales events, machine alerts, and low-stock warnings. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device's notification settings. Disabling notifications will not affect your access to the App but may delay awareness of important machine events.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase, payment processors). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. Vending King is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

9. Children's Privacy

The Vending King App is intended for use by business operators and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, for material changes, notify you via the App or email. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
