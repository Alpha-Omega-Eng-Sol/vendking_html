FLEET CONTROL

Machine Management

Monitor your entire fleet from one screen. View machine locations, check stock levels, and monitor sales — no matter how many machines you operate.

  • See and monitor machine locations and real-time activity
  • Check machine stock levels and manage fill schedules
  • Location-based machine tracking
STAY INFORMED

Instant Notifications

Stay ahead of issues before they become problems. Receive real-time alerts for sales, technical faults, or low stock — all from your home screen shortcuts.

  • Push notifications for every sale
  • Instant alerts for all machines
  • Quick shortcuts for critical actions
STOCK CONTROL

Product Management

Keep your machines fully stocked and running at peak performance. Easily add products, set prices, and view product-level inventory from anywhere.

  • Easily add new products to any machine
  • Set prices and cost prices for profit tracking
  • Visual inventory levels and restocking controls
FULL HISTORY

Purchase Logs

Track every transaction with a monthly breakdown. Know exactly what's selling, when, and how much revenue it generates — down to the specific deal.

  • Daily transaction logs
  • Item-level purchase details
  • Drill down into specific deals
DATA INSIGHTS

Sales Analytics

Track your revenue, profit, and sales performance. Filter by machine or time period for detailed insights. Real-time data to help you make smarter business decisions.

  • Monthly and yearly profit summaries

  • Filter data by machine, period, and time
