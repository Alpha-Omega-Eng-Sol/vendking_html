IoT Dashboard: Managing Multiple Machines Remotely

Learn how LphaVend's IoT dashboard helps you monitor inventory, track sales, and manage multiple locations from anywhere — perfect for scaling in Nepal.

LphaVend Team
• February 8, 2026

Running 5+ vending machines becomes significantly easier with real-time remote monitoring. LphaVend's IoT dashboard gives operators full visibility and control from a phone or laptop — anywhere with internet.

Key Features of the LphaVend IoT Dashboard

  • Real-time inventory levels per product slot
  • Low-stock & out-of-stock alerts (SMS + app push)
  • Daily / weekly / monthly sales reports
  • Payment breakdown (cash vs digital)
  • Machine status (online/offline, temperature, errors)
  • Heatmap of peak sales hours & best-selling items

How It Helps in Nepal

In areas with frequent power cuts or difficult road access, remote monitoring reduces unnecessary site visits by 60–80%. Operators can plan restocking trips efficiently and respond quickly to issues before sales are lost.

Getting Started

  1. All LphaVend machines come IoT-ready (SIM card included)
  2. Create account at dashboard.lphavend.com
  3. Machines auto-register when powered on
  4. Set custom alert thresholds (e.g., <20% stock → notify)

Businesses managing 10+ machines report saving 15–25 hours per week in travel and checking time.
